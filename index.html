About Kamgaar

Who We Are

Kamgaar is a workforce management company built exclusively for heavy-equipment operators. We employ operators full-time, manage them end-to-end, and deploy them to critical infrastructure, construction, industrial, and mining projects across India.

Heavy equipment cannot afford downtime ‚Äî and neither can the businesses that rely on it. Kamgaar exists to eliminate manpower uncertainty from mission-critical operations.

What We Do

We Employ Operators Full-Time

All operators on Kamgaar are hired as permanent employees. They receive fixed salaries, formal employment contracts, PF, insurance, and statutory benefits ‚Äî creating stability, accountability, and long-term commitment.

We Deploy Operators to Client Sites

Our operators are deployed to EPCs, contractors, and equipment rental companies based on the right skill, right machine, and right site requirements. Clients get ready-to-work operators without the burden of hiring, payroll, or compliance.

We Guarantee Workforce Continuity

We provide SLA-backed replacements to ensure zero downtime for critical equipment. If an operator is unavailable, Kamgaar manages rapid replacement ‚Äî so operations never stop.
